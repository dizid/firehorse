## TECHNICAL SPECIFICATION

### Recommended ESP (Free-Tier Options)

**Option 1: Resend (Recommended)**
- **Why:** Developer-friendly, generous free tier (100 emails/day), excellent deliverability
- **Free tier:** 3,000 emails/month, 100/day limit
- **Pricing:** Free tier ‚Üí $20/month for 50,000 emails
- **Setup:** React/TypeScript SDK available, Netlify Functions integration
- **Best for:** Solo founders, technical teams

**Option 2: Brevo (formerly Sendinblue)**
- **Why:** 300 emails/day free tier, built-in marketing automation
- **Free tier:** 300 emails/day (9,000/month), unlimited contacts
- **Pricing:** Free tier ‚Üí $25/month for 20,000 emails
- **Setup:** Webhook integration, visual automation builder
- **Best for:** Non-technical users, visual workflow preference

**Option 3: Mailchimp**
- **Why:** Industry standard, robust analytics, familiar interface
- **Free tier:** 500 contacts, 1,000 emails/month (very limited)
- **Pricing:** Free tier ‚Üí $13/month for 500 contacts
- **Setup:** Form integration, automation builder
- **Best for:** Established brands, budget for paid tier

**Verdict:** Use Resend for technical control + cost efficiency. Use Brevo if you need visual automation builder and higher free tier send limits.

**ERROR HANDLING:**

- **If email bounces (hard bounce):** Remove from list, flag email as invalid
- **If email bounces (soft bounce):** Retry 3x over 24 hours ‚Üí mark as soft-bounce if all fail
